Arcade Guide

  • ArcadeBot Notes
  • ArcadeBot Rewards

Weekly Rewards: Every Friday, TOP 10 Arcade members from both Weekly Messages and Weekly Wins categories are rewarded.

Important Note: Players who rank TOP 10 in both categories will only be eligible to claim a reward from one category — the higher placement.

Monthly Cap: Each member's monthly winnings from ArcadeBot are capped at 15,000 coins - totals reset back to zero at the start of each month.

Reward Options: Winners may optionally claim Pips-exclusive items; however, it's the coin value that still counts toward their monthly total.
